BACKUP AND DISASTER RECOVERY PROCEDURES

Backup Strategy:
SecureSync performs automated daily backups at 00:00 UTC. Incremental backups run every 6 hours. All backups are encrypted with AES-256 before transmission. Backup retention follows the 3-2-1 rule: 3 copies, 2 different media types, 1 offsite.

Geographic Redundancy:
Primary data center: US-East (Virginia)
Secondary data center: US-West (Oregon)
Tertiary data center: EU-West (Ireland)
All data centers are SOC 2 Type II certified. Automatic failover is configured for critical systems.

Recovery Point Objective (RPO):
The RPO is 1 hour for all customer data. This means maximum data loss of 1 hour in case of disaster. Transaction logs are backed up every 15 minutes. Database replication is near real-time.

Recovery Time Objective (RTO):
The RTO is 4 hours for critical services. Non-critical services restore within 24 hours. Documented runbooks enable rapid recovery. Regular testing ensures RTO is achievable.

Disaster Recovery Testing:
Annual disaster recovery drills are conducted. Last successful test: All systems restored within 2.5 hours. Failover testing performed quarterly. Business continuity plan reviewed annually.

Data Restoration Procedures:
Automated restoration from backup is available. Manual restoration takes 4-8 hours depending on data size. Point-in-time recovery supported within 7 days. Emergency restoration available 24/7 via support ticket.

Business Continuity:
Hot standby infrastructure is maintained. Load balancing distributes traffic across regions. Automatic scaling handles traffic spikes. DDoS protection via Cloudflare.
