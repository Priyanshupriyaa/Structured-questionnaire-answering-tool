SECURITY ASSESSMENT QUESTIONNAIRE

2. How is data encrypted during transmission?
3. What authentication methods do you support?

5. How are audit logs maintained and protected?
6. What is your data retention policy?
7. How do you handle incident response?
8. What compliance certifications do you hold?
9. How often do you perform security audits?
10. What is your backup and disaster recovery process?
